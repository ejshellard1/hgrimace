H.GRIMACE

H. Grimace are: Corin Johnson (Bass), Diogo Gomez (Drums), Hannah Gledhill (Guitars, Vocals), Patrick Browne (Guitars).

Review of debut LP SELF ARCHITECT -"Named after the snarl that characterises singer Hannah Gledhill’s demeanour and which partially accounts for the bruising nature of her bands heavy sneaking. London four-piece H.Grimace look to the wiry dissonance of Sonic Youth’s Sister as a blueprint for their excellent debut, Self Architect. Gledhill and fellow guitarist Marcus Browne are an intuitive partnership with a gift for nifty songwriting, as "Call it Out" and "Land/Body” show. But they’re most interesting when they push themselves on the nuanced sprawl of "2.1 Woman” and “The Dial”, where Gledhill questions her circumstances and the band unravel some deeply satisfying Slint-ish shoe gaze." -Piers Martin (UNCUT Magazine) 2017
